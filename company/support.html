Support for Fail Better Productions

Fail Better wishes to thank A7 Communications, Pegasus Theatre and The CAPITAL Centre for their continued support.

On 8th September 2007, we held our first Gala Evening for Fail Better Friends. This exclusive event, held at the Rosemary Branch Theatre, featured performances of Diary of a Madman, Stasis: Beckett Shorts and a themed drinks reception. The Fail Better company at the Gala Evening

The challenge for young theatre companies to get a foot on the funding ladder still remains and recent cuts suggest a bleak future for independent arts organisations. Nonetheless, we find a way to keep failing better and struggle somehow to fund each stage of our development as a company. We aim to demonstrate our creative and critical worth over these difficult foundation years and one day secure long term funding. If you would like to support the company at a crucial stage in our development, please email our Artistic Director Jonathan Heron at failbetterproductions@hotmail.com.

Try again. Fail again. Fail better.
Samuel Beckett, Worstward Ho
